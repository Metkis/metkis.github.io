OUR MISSION

Promote. Provide. Protect.

Black Lives Matter imagines a world where Black people across the world thrive, experience joy, and are not defined by their struggles. In pursuing liberation, we envision a future fully divested from police, prisons, and all punishment paradigms and which invests in justice, joy, and culture.

BLACK LIVES MATTER

South Bend

2013 TO PRESENT

In 2013, three radical Black organizers — Alicia Garza, Patrisse Cullors, and Opal Tometi — created a Black-centered political-movement-building project called #BlackLivesMatter in response to the acquittal of Trayvon Martin’s murderer, George Zimmerman.

Their work sparked a groundroots movement across the US to seek equity and justice for Black Americans. In 20XX, [Founder] and [Co-Founder] built Black Lives Matter South Bend in an effort to bring accountability to the South Bend Police and provide justice for the countless assaults and rights violations that the Black persons of South Bend face daily.
